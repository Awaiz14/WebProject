A little bit about us...

Who We Are

WeLoveFood is a community for food enthusiasts who share a common love for different foods and flavours.

What We Do

At WeLoveFood we deliver mouth-watering recipes and provide food related inspiration for our fellow foodies.

Our Main Goal

At WeLoveFood our aim is to bring food lovers together and make the kitchen your favourite place.

Our story...

WeLoveFood was founded in 2023 by Awaiz Solkar, a true food lover
with a zest for culinary exploration. With a mission to
celebrate diverse flavours and unique dining experiences, Awaiz
and his team set out on a journey to capture hearts and taste buds
of food enthusiasts worldwide. It all started off with a food tour
in Manchester, a multi-cultural city in England with a thousand
different flavours. From luxurious restaurants to local streetfoods,
a delightful array of dishes were explored.
Thus came the idea of bringing these dishes right to your home.
Through the universal language of food, WeLoveFood set out to
provide a platform where food lovers could find a wide range
of delicious recipes to suit every taste and occasion. This way
our foodies can find all sorts of international flavours all in
one place and enjoy every bite till the end. Bon Appetit!
